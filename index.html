News Classifier

Automated topic understanding for news streams.

Problem Statement

Manual categorization of news articles is slow and inconsistent. Stakeholders needed real-time, multi-class classification to power personalization & analytics.

Key Features / Techniques

  • Data pipeline with text normalization, tokenization & padding.
  • LSTM-based architecture (Keras) with embedding layer & dropout regularization.
  • Model checkpointing & early stopping for generalization.
  • Evaluation: accuracy, F1 per class & confusion matrix visualization.
  • Exported as a reusable inference module (saved Keras model).

Tech Stack

Languages: Python. Frameworks: TensorFlow / Keras. Libraries: NumPy, Pandas, scikit-learn. Other: SavedModel deployment, NumPy datasets.

  • Fallback handling & intent confidence thresholding (extensible).
